Ghostly Kisses

ABOUT


Margaux Sauvé, the talent behind Ghostly Kisses, has crafted a distinct and mesmerizing musical identity. Growing up in Quebec City, Canada, she was immersed in a rich musical environment from a young age, thanks in part to her family’s musical inclinations. Her early years were spent learning classical violin, an experience that deeply influenced her understanding of music and honed her technical skills.

After discovering her passion for singing and songwriting, Sauvé began experimenting with different genres and sounds, ultimately leading to the formation of Ghostly Kisses. Her stage name, drawn from a poignant line in a William Faulkner poem, reflects the evocative and ethereal nature of her music. This project allowed her to blend her classical background with contemporary influences, resulting in a unique sound that is at once nostalgic and modern.

Sauvé’s musical journey has been marked by a series of well-received releases that have steadily built her reputation. Her debut EP, "What You See," introduced listeners to her hauntingly beautiful style, characterized by lush instrumentals and introspective lyrics. This was followed by additional EPs and singles, each showcasing her growth as an artist and her ability to convey deep emotions through her music.

In live performances, Sauvé’s ethereal presence and captivating voice create an intimate atmosphere, drawing audiences into the world of Ghostly Kisses. Her ability to connect with listeners on an emotional level is one of her defining traits, and her concerts often feel like a shared, almost mystical experience.

Beyond her recorded music and live performances, Sauvé is also known for her collaborations with other artists and producers, which have enriched her sound and expanded her creative horizons. These collaborations often bring new dimensions to her music, blending her signature style with diverse influences.

As Ghostly Kisses, Margaux Sauvé continues to enchant listeners around the world. Her music, with its delicate balance of melancholy and hope, offers a refuge for those seeking solace and beauty. Sauvé’s dedication to her craft and her ability to evoke deep emotions ensure that Ghostly Kisses will remain a cherished and influential presence in the music world for years to come.
